Our Mission

To eradicate hunger and minimize food waste through community-driven technology.

The Problem

Every year, millions of tons of perfectly good food are thrown away globally, all while millions of people go to bed hungry. This massive disconnect in the food supply chain contributes heavily to greenhouse gas emissions and environmental degradation, all while local communities unnecessarily suffer.

Our Solution

Food Reuse bridges the gap between those who have surplus food and those who need it. Our platform allows local restaurants, bakeries, event organizers, and individuals to instantly notify local NGOs and charities when perfectly good food is available for pickup.

Join the Movement

Whether you're a donor looking to give back, or an organization that distributes to those in need, your participation makes a direct impact. Together, we can ensure every meal counts and zero food ends up in landfills.
